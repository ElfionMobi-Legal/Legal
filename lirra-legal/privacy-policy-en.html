Important — not a medical product: Lirra’s Calm Score is a habit score based on your morning protocol, self-report, and streak. It is not a laboratory measurement, hormone test, cortisol reading, medical diagnosis, or treatment. The App is for personal wellness and habit support only.

3. Information we collect

3.1 Information you provide

  • Onboarding answers. Age band, selected symptoms/habits, goal, usual wake time, and optional display name used for greetings and personalization.
  • Ritual inputs. Which protocol steps you complete or skip, optional self-report (how you feel), and optional soft sleep quality (rough / ok / rested).
  • Support communications. If you email us, we process your email address and message content to respond.

3.2 Information created by the App and our servers

  • User identifiers. On first use we typically create an anonymous authentication session (Supabase Auth). That ID links your profile, ritual days, streak, and subscription status. Optional Sign in with Apple / Google may be offered later to upgrade the same account.
  • Scores & history. Calm Score, score band, streak counters, day keys, and related breakdowns stored so you can see progress across devices/sessions.
  • Technical request metadata. Timestamps, request IDs, approximate IP for security/rate limiting, and error codes as needed to operate the API.

3.3 On-device data (generally not uploaded as content archives)

  • Journal (optional one-line note): stored locally on your device for the day; not designed as a cloud journal sync.
  • Preferences: theme, language override, night-prep nudge flag, card/widget style, onboarding completion.
  • Local notifications: wake-window and optional evening reminders scheduled on-device (no marketing push server in MVP).
  • Home-screen widget cache: recent Calm Score / streak shown via App Group / App Widget storage on device.

3.4 Purchases and entitlements

If you subscribe to Lirra Pro (weekly, monthly, or yearly), payment is processed by Apple or Google. We receive entitlement/status information through RevenueCat and/or store APIs so we can unlock Pro features (for example Night Prep, NSDR deep rest, themes). We do not receive or store your full payment card number.

3.5 Optional analytics

When Firebase Analytics / Crashlytics is configured in a release build, we may process aggregated usage events, device model, OS version, app version, crash stacks, and instance identifiers as processed by Google under their terms. If those SDKs are not enabled, we do not send that analytics traffic.

3.6 Data we do not collect (MVP)

  • HealthKit / Health Connect clinical metrics, heart rate, or lab values
  • Camera, microphone, contacts, precise GPS for advertising
  • Advertising ID for cross-app tracking; we do not run third-party ad networks in the App
  • Permanent voice or video recordings

4. How we use information

  • To build your personal morning protocol and display Calm Score / streak
  • To schedule local wake and optional evening reminders you enable
  • To unlock and restore Lirra Pro entitlements
  • To secure the service (authentication, rate limiting, abuse prevention)
  • To fix bugs, measure reliability, and improve the product
  • To respond to support and deletion requests and meet legal obligations

We do not sell your personal information. We do not use your ritual answers to train unrelated third-party foundation models unless we clearly disclose a change and obtain any consent required by law.

5. Legal bases (EEA/UK and similar regimes)

Where GDPR-style rules apply, we process data under one or more of: performance of a contract (providing the App you requested); legitimate interests (security, product improvement, fraud prevention—balanced against your rights); consent where required (e.g., notification permission); and legal obligation when we must retain or disclose information.

6. Sharing with service providers

We share data with vendors who process it on our instructions to operate Lirra:

  • Cloudflare — API hosting (Workers), rate-limit KV, security
  • Supabase — authentication and database (profiles, ritual days, streaks, subscriptions)
  • RevenueCat — subscription entitlement management
  • Apple / Google — app distribution, billing, and (if used) Sign in with Apple / Google
  • Google Firebase (optional) — analytics and crash reporting

These providers may process data in the United States or other countries. Where required, we rely on appropriate transfer mechanisms offered by those providers (such as Standard Contractual Clauses).

We may also disclose information if required by law, to protect rights and safety, or in connection with a merger, acquisition, or asset sale (with notice where required).

7. Retention

  • Profile, ritual history, streak: retained while your account remains active and as needed to provide the service
  • Subscription records: retained as needed for accounting, fraud prevention, and legal compliance
  • On-device prefs / journal: until you clear app data, uninstall, or delete your account (server wipe does not always clear every local file until reinstall)
  • Support emails: retained as needed to resolve your request

Uninstalling the App removes local device data but does not automatically erase server-side records tied to your user ID. Use in-app Settings → Delete account or contact us as described below / on our Account Deletion page.
