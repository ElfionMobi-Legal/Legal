Request Account Deletion

Timbr · Elfion Mobile · Package app.timbr.timbr

Use this page to request deletion of your Timbr account and associated data. You do not need to reinstall the app.

How to request deletion

  1. Send an email to elfionmobi@gmail.com
  2. Use subject line: Delete Account — Timbr
  3. Include if you have them:
    • Platform (Android / iOS)
    • Approximate first-use date
    • Any user ID shown in Timbr → Settings (if available)
    • The Google Play / App Store email you used for purchases (if any)

Email deletion request

In the app

You can also start deletion from Timbr: Settings → Delete account.

What we delete

  • Your Timbr account / anonymous user ID
  • Voice scan results, scores, plans, and related profile preferences stored for that account
  • Any temporary audio still pending analysis (audio is normally deleted after scoring)

What may be retained

  • Limited subscription / purchase records needed for accounting, fraud prevention, or legal compliance (we do not store your full payment card number)
  • Support email correspondence as needed to complete your request

Canceling a Timbr Pro subscription is managed in Google Play or the App Store; deletion of your Timbr account does not by itself cancel store billing.

Timing

We typically acknowledge requests within 24–72 hours and complete deletion within a reasonable period (and as required by applicable law).
