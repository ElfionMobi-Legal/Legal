🔒 Privacy Policy

CaughtU is built privacy-first. The app runs entirely on your device, does not require an account, and never uploads your photos or protected app list to any server. This privacy policy explains exactly what data the app handles and how it is protected.

📱 No Account, No Sign-In

CaughtU does not require you to create an account or sign in. We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or any directly identifying information to use the core features of the app. All protection settings live on your device.

📦 Information Stored On Your Device

To provide its core service, CaughtU stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Protected App List: The apps you choose to protect
  • Security Settings: Your PIN or pattern (stored securely on-device), biometric preference, and the selected secret unlock gesture
  • Intruder Evidence Photos: Front-camera selfies captured after failed unlock attempts
  • App Preferences: Selected fake-screen theme, language, dark mode, and other settings
  • Attempt Logs: Timestamps and counts of unlock attempts shown in your reports

Important: This data never leaves your device. Photos and your protected app list are not uploaded to the cloud and are not accessible to us.

📷 Camera and Intruder Selfies

When the intruder selfie feature is enabled, CaughtU uses the front camera to silently capture a photo after a failed unlock attempt on a protected app. These photos:

  • Are stored only in the app's private storage on your device
  • Are never transmitted to us or any third party
  • Can be viewed and deleted by you at any time from the Reports screen
  • Are permanently removed when you delete them or uninstall the app

♿ Accessibility Service

CaughtU uses Android's Accessibility Service for a single, narrow purpose: to detect when a protected app is brought to the foreground so the protection screen can be displayed.

The Accessibility Service NEVER:

  • Reads, records, or stores the content of any screen
  • Logs keystrokes, passwords, or messages
  • Performs actions automatically on your behalf
  • Collects personal or browsing data

🎯 How We Use Data

The on-device data described above is used only to:

  • Detect access to protected apps and display the fake/protection screen
  • Authenticate you via your secret gesture and PIN, pattern, or biometric
  • Capture and show intruder evidence (when enabled)
  • Show your security score, reports, and recent attempts
  • Remember your preferences (theme, language, appearance)

📊 Diagnostics and Analytics

To keep the app stable and improve it, CaughtU may use the following Google services that collect aggregated and anonymized data:

  • Firebase Analytics: Anonymized usage statistics (e.g., which screens are opened, feature usage)
  • Firebase Crashlytics: Crash and error reports to help us fix bugs
  • Firebase Remote Config: Feature configuration

These services may process device information such as device model, operating system version, app version, and a non-permanent identifier. This data is not used to personally identify you.

📣 Advertising

The free version of CaughtU may show ads through Google AdMob. To serve ads, AdMob may collect and use an advertising identifier and limited device information in accordance with Google's policies. Premium (paid) users do not see ads. You can manage or reset your advertising ID in your device settings.

💳 Purchases

Premium subscriptions and one-time purchases are processed through Google Play Billing and managed via RevenueCat. We receive your subscription/entitlement status and anonymous transaction identifiers for purchase verification and restore. We do not receive or store your credit card or payment details — payment is handled entirely by Google Play.

🤝 Third-Party Services

CaughtU relies on the following third-party services, each governed by its own privacy policy:

  • Google Firebase (Analytics, Crashlytics, Remote Config)
  • Google AdMob (advertising, free version only)
  • Google Play Billing (payment processing)
  • RevenueCat (subscription management)

We recommend reviewing their privacy policies to understand how they handle data.

🔐 Data Security

  • Sensitive data such as your unlock code is stored in the app's private, protected storage
  • Intruder photos are kept in app-private storage that other apps cannot access
  • Network communication with third-party services uses encrypted HTTPS connections
  • No personal evidence data (photos, app list) is transmitted off your device

👶 Children's Privacy

CaughtU is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at elfionmobi@gmail.com.

🗑️ Data Retention and Deletion

Because your data is stored locally, you are in full control:

  • Delete individual intruder photos from the Reports screen at any time
  • Uninstalling the app permanently removes all local data, including photos, settings, and logs
  • To opt out of personalized ads or reset your advertising identifier, use your device's privacy settings

📝 Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to access, correct, or delete your data. Since CaughtU stores data on your device and does not maintain accounts, you can exercise most of these rights directly in the app or by uninstalling it. For requests regarding data processed by our diagnostics providers, contact us at elfionmobi@gmail.com.
