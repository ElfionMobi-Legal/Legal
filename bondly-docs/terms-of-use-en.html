📱 App Description

Bondly is a multiplayer habit-building game disguised as a cute emotional pet app. The app offers the following features:

  • Create and track personal habits (water, reading, exercise, journaling, steps, etc.)
  • Join rooms with your partner, friends, or play solo
  • Share an emotional pet that reacts to your habit completion and failures
  • Experience emotional accountability when your habits affect your partner's pet
  • View event feed narrating your journey and pet reactions
  • Unlock multiple pet types and skins
  • Premium features and in-app purchases (paid)
